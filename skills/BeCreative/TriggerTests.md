# Trigger Tests: BeCreative

## Should Trigger
1. "be creative and come up with an unusual angle for this blog post" - "be creative" is the primary trigger phrase; routes to StandardCreativity or MaximumCreativity workflow
2. "I need deep thinking on this complex architecture decision" - "deep thinking" is a direct trigger; engages extended reasoning for quality creative output
3. "brainstorm radically different approaches to this onboarding flow" - "brainstorm" + "radically different" triggers IdeaGeneration or MaximumCreativity workflow
4. "use extended reasoning to explore this multi-factor problem" - "extended reasoning" is a direct trigger; routes to TreeOfThoughts for branching exploration
5. "think outside the box for naming this security product" - Creative ideation request with "think outside the box" triggers StandardCreativity or MaximumCreativity

## Should NOT Trigger
1. "red team this proposal and find every weakness" - Correct skill: RedTeam (adversarial critique with 32 agents, not creative ideation)
2. "break this problem down to first principles" - Correct skill: FirstPrinciples (systematic decomposition, not creative exploration)
3. "research creative writing techniques used by famous authors" - Correct skill: Research (researching the topic of creativity, not applying creative thinking)
4. "create a visualization of this data" - Correct skill: Art (visual content creation, not creative thinking/brainstorming)
5. "generate a header image with an artistic style" - Correct skill: Art (image generation, not creative reasoning or idea generation)
